There are a few upcoming public events scheduled for Dr. Jordan B Peterson which you can check on the table below. The current COVID-19 pandemic makes any new public appearances very difficult due to all the demanded protocols and restrictions. The 12 Rules Tour came to an end on February 2019, having covered some 160 cities in the US, Canada, Europe, Australia and New Zealand (attracting a total audience of 500,000), following the debut of 12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos in January of 2018. Dave Rubin of The Rubin Report opened for many of the shows. The best of them have already been or will be posted on my podcast in the near future.

Thank you to all of those who attended in the past.
